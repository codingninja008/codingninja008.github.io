This page outlines the activities and competitions participated.

2017

NASA Space Apps Challenge

This was the first Hackathon competition participated at Petrosains, Suria KLCC. Presented a topic about an app relating to detecting water levels at beaches and notifying users of UV-ray levels.

2017

Virtual Reality ASEAN Hack
(VRA)

Participated in Malaysian Communications and Multimedia Commission (MCMC), Cyberjaya. Presented a Crime Scene Investigation (CSI) game based approach solution in Virtual Reality (VR). Achieved and won the Ideathon category.

2018

SMartER Hackathon Penang

Presented an idea about a game app consisting of Pokemon-Go features inviting users to travel physically to places of interest and collecting badges.

2018

NASA Space Apps Challenge

This Hackathon was held at Petrosains, Suria KLCC. Presented a framework design for autonomous free-flyer to inspect a spacecraft for damage from Micro-Meteoroid and Orbital Debris (MMOD).

2019

Global Hackathon
"Reboot The Earth"

This hackathon was held at UN Technology Innovation Labs (Sunway iLabs). Presented a farmework incorporating Internet of Things(IOT) and Unmanned Aerial Vehicle (UAV) to combat fire incidents.

2019

NASA Space Apps Challenge

Presented a topic about a game app relating to teaching users about James Webb Space Telescope's mission.

This page outlines the activities and competitions participated.

NASA Space Apps Challenge 2017

This was the first Hackathon competition participated and it was held at Petrosains, Suria KLCC. Presented a topic about an app relating to detecting water levels at beaches and notifying users of UV-ray levels.

Virtual Reality ASEAN Hack (VRA) 2017

This hackathon was held at Malaysian Communications and Multimedia Commission (MCMC), Cyberjaya. The challenge in this Hackathon competition was an open category and was divided into Hackathon and Ideathon category. Presented a topic about having a Crime Scene Investigation (CSI) game based approach in Virtual Reality (VR). Achieved and won the Ideathon category.

Presented an idea about a game app consisting of Pokemon-Go features inviting users to travel physically to places of interest and collecting badges.
